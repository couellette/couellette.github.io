SKILLS & EXPERTISE

I've been developing my skill–set to focus on what's most suited to getting the job done quickly and effectively.

  • Front-end Development

    Whether it's websites, custom HTML letters, or even enterprise level frontend development of applications, I can certainly help.
    I write code that conforms to the W3C's web standards and best practices. I use HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery, to transform ideas into mobile first, elegant, interactive and fully functional user-interfaces; exactly as it was initially envisioned.

  • User Experience

    It is far better to adapt the technology to the user than to force the user to adapt to the technology.
    For me, it is not always the primary goal of simply making a polished user-interface. A truly successful, website or application requires usability testing, UX interview and research to validate ideas with your target audience.

  • User Interface Design

    Design is a skill that cannot be acquired with education alone, but with years of experience, keeping up with industry trends, and creative thinking.
    If the end product is not perfect - I am not satisfied. I couple my UX experience with UI to create a seamless overall experience to not only bring great visual UI, but the right end product for your audience.

  • Responsive Web Design

    The web is no longer built with only the desktop computer in mind. Today we also have an array of smartphones and tablet devices, with much more to come.
    I take an unorthodox approach to responsive design because each website/application I make, I write the entire responsive framework from scratch.
    No cookie-cutter design - ever.

  • WordPress Integration

    I can help you leverage one of the world's most popular and powerful content management systems; tailoring it specifically to your needs.
    WordPress has become the simplest and quickest way for me to develop new, fully content managed websites. However, integrating a fully custom coded site or application into the CMS is a skill which I shine.

  • Organic SEO/SEM

    You can have the best website in the world, but without visibility you may as well not even be there.
    I have helped companies leverage Organic SEO techniques to help ensure they show up in top page search results but using SEO best practices, social integration, and coding markup to ensure success.
